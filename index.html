Home Row Basics

  • A S D F are typed by your left hand.
  • J K L ; are typed by your right hand.
  • Your index fingers rest on F and J.
  • Keep your wrists relaxed and sit up straight.
  • Try to look at the screen instead of the keyboard.

Finger Guide

  • Left pinky: A
  • Left ring finger: S
  • Left middle finger: D
  • Left index finger: F and nearby keys
  • Right index finger: J and nearby keys
  • Right middle finger: K
  • Right ring finger: L
  • Right pinky: ;
